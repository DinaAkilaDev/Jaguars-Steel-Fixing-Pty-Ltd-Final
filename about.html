More Than 3 Years Experience

Jaguars Steel Fixing is Victoria’s leading supplier for all your steel fixing needs. Since it’s inception in 2020, we have managed and completed small, medium and commercial projects within Victoria. Our strong focus on SAFETY has set us well ahead of our competitors. Pride in our craftsmanship and attention to detail is what we deliver day in day out to all our clients and projects. With the one of the largest manpower crew currently in Victoria, Jaguars Steel Fixing is able to offer end to end solution for all your steel fixing requirements. We strive for loyalty, trust and perfection in any project we take on
